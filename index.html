When was masala dosa invented?

The masala dosa made in the Udupi style is generally thin, crispy, and filled with a spicy potato mixture. while others would say, the masala dosa was created in the Karnataka city of Dharwad. This idea holds that the masala dosa was created in the early 20th century at a nearby restaurant called Ratna Cafe.
